About Me

From the very first instance that I embarked on the journey of studying English, I ineluctably fell in love with it and anything related to its origins. What's more, as a kid I was absolutely riveted on my English teacher's lessons, and felt that my thirst for knowledge just kept growing bigger and bigger! As a result, I have grown attached ESL teaching, with the purpose of transmitting my passion for the language to other people.

Although I have prior experience as a substitute teacher and private tutor, my formal teaching career started as soon as I turned 18. As a former game developer, I have also found a way to complement my teaching with coding skills to design fun activities for my students. Whether it be a Minecraft map or an interactive game, they always love it!

Exam Preparation

My biggest goal is working for Cambridge as an evaluator. For this reason, I undergo exhaustive exam self-training on a daily basis, which epitomizes my profile as an exam teacher. In addition to this, I have taken several English tests, including many previews and mock exams, now with a current focus on the Cambridge C2 exam. I have taught successful students in most of the following exams:

Cambridge English: C2 Proficiency 210/230
Cambridge English: B2 First 182/190
Duolingo English Test 145/160 (Preview)
iTEP Academic Plus5.5/6.0 (Practice test)
